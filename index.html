Who Is Kenan?



Kenan Deari grew up the youngest of two children. Being in a family of first-generation immigrants in America that moved from Bosnia to Dallas in 1998, I didn't know what to expect. The struggles of fitting in, learning to speak & write English were difficult but helped craft me in the man I'm today. It wasn't long before he made friends with almost anyone he interacted with due to his confident, friendly, and funny personality. Kenan was always involved in sports, he played soccer, basketball, and football till graduation & had teams outside of school.

Kenan spent his childhood growing up in Plano, Texas. He was enrolled in Plano ISD schools from K-12. Kenan graduated from Plano Senior High School ahead of schedule for the class of 2017. After graduation, he assisted and helped run a family-owned corner store and also worked for a friends business.

Kenan is currently employed for Crest Cadillac in Frisco, Texas. His role is the Loan Car Manager. His title consists of maintaining a fleet of Cadillac vehicles, dispatch drivers & ensure vehicles are ready for scheduled valets on weekdays (6-8 per day), manage & schedule 10 employees, and ensure exceptional service for all our guests. One of the most significant implications Kenan provided for Crest Cadillac was the deployment and understanding of a new software to assist correctly calculating toll-roads and fuel charges that customers had due. The change Kenan provided helped solve an on-going issue the service department faced.

Because of his desire to understand and become a successful web developer, Kenan enrolled himself in the Full Stack Web Development program offered by Southern Methodist University. Kenan will come out of the program with strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript. He will also be equipped with experience working with Bootstrap, React.js, Node.js, MySQL, and much more. Kenan's a firm believer that you can never learn enough. He's extremely dedicated, passionate, and detailed in his work. He never settles & always strives to grow.

Following Kenan's graduation, he will be seeking a role for an entry or junior level front end development, back-end development, or JavaScript development roles. The experience & skills learned in SMU's Full-Stack program have prepared Kenan to prosper in any environment. Paired with the personal drive, mindset, and resilience that Kenan possesses, he believes the sky is the limit.
